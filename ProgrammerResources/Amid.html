Python

Codecademy:

Codecademy is a great resource for learning to code most any language.
Their Python 3 course is an amazing one for beginners, as it showcases how easy python is to learn,
as well as the methods used throughout most of their courses.

W3Schools

W3Schools is another amazing resource for programmers.
Although they mostly focus on Web Development, they also have pages and tutorials on python.
These are more about reading and examples than having you type yourself, but they are
still a great way to find what you are looking for.

TutorialsPoint

TutorialsPoint is a good all around resource, but they mostly focus on the textbook
of the language. This leads to it being a bit more boring to read, but still great
if you have the time and dedication to read it all.



Conclusion

This is not meant to be the end all of python. These are just the tip of the iceberg with what you can do,
and I encourage you to look elsewhere to find information. Google is your best friend.
